Offered Services

At the Temple Inn and Suites we want you to provide you with the best possible experience during as you plan your trip to the temple. That's why we offer specialized services that will make your stay with us easy and as comfortable as possible.

Provided Amenities

  • Breakfast
  • Exercise Room
  • Indoor Pool
  • WiFi high-speed Internet Access
  • Smart TV's
  • Parking
  • Temple Shuttle
  • Family History Consultatant
  • Family Ordinance Card Service

Specialized Services

  • Access to changing rooms and bathroom facilities for temple patrons who need to change to and from church clothes.
  • Low-cost, short-term child care services with a playroom beautiful playroom for the children of patrons.
  • Long-term living accommodations for full-time missionaries serving at the temple. These rooms feature a kitchenette and a small living room.
  • A beautiful wedding reception hall that can be easily customized to fit the needs of the wedding party. We also offer a simple catering menu and kitchen access for food preparation and serving.
  • A family search research center equipped with computers and friendly knowledgeable staff trained to assist you in your family search and ordinance work.
