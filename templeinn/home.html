Welcome to the Temple Inn and Suites

The Temple Inn and Suites provides a beautiful, comfortable, and peaceful place for members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints to stay when attending or serving in the temple. Our is pleased to provide specialized services unique to temple attendance such as a shuttle service, child care, and changing rooms to temple patrons. Allow us to serve you and your temple attendance needs at the Temple Inn and Suites.

Saratoga Springs

The Saratoga Springs Utah Temple was announced on 2 April 2017. The groundbreaking and Site dedication took place on 19 October 2019 by Elder Craig C. Christensen. This temple is not yet complete, but is scheduled to be dedicated sometime in 2022.
