Full-Time Temple Missionaries

We are grateful for your desire to serve the Lord as full-time missionaries in the temple. The Temple Inn and Suites offers one bedroom and two bedroom suites with extended living space for Long-term living. We want you to have all the comforts of home while serving away from home.

One Bedroom Suite

  • 600 square feet of living space
  • Sleeps 1-4 people with sofa sleeper
  • Bedroom is separate from living room and kitchen
  • Spacious living room with sofa bed
  • Fully equipped kitchen with dining room table
  • Wood floors throughout kitchen and living space
  • Office space and desk in the bedroom
  • Beautiful bathroom, with large shower and his and her sinks
  • Luxury bedding and linens
  • Free WiFi Access
  • TV's in living room and the bedroom
  • Private Balcony

Two Bedroom Suite

  • 750 square feet of living space
  • Sleeps4-6 people
  • Bedrooms are separate from the living room and kitchen
  • Spacious living room with sofa bed
  • Fully equipped kitchen with dining room table
  • Wood floors throughout kitchen and living space
  • Office space and desk in the bedroom
  • Beautiful bathroom, with large shower and his and her sinks
  • Luxury bedding and linens
  • Free WiFi Access
  • TV's in living room and the bedrooms
  • Private Balcony
