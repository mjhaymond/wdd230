Receptions

The Temple Inn and Suites knows how to do weddings! We want your special day to be exactly as you've always imagined it. Our hotel facilities offer three different venue location's on the hotel grounds that can be customized to fit your preferred style. The Grand ballroom, a white tent reception area, and our garden reception area.

Wedding Package Amenities

  • White linen tablecloths and a wide selection of colorful linen napkins and table runners.
  • White china tableware, stemmed glassware and flatware.
  • Room set-up including a head table for your wedding party, banquet rounds for your guests, wood dance floor for the tent and garden receptions areas, staging for your band or DJ, gift tables, and the cake display table.
  • A variety of flowers and decor items for table centerpieces.
  • A professional florist, and decorating staff who will transform our venues to meet your wedding style and taste.
  • A full-staff catering kitchen, with professional chefs, pastry chefs, and cake decorators.
  • A professional banquet service staff ready to serve your plated dinners and/or replenish the buffet areas.
  • Meal options include beef brisket, chicken, or fish, with a variety of side dishes to choose from.
  • Plenty of free self-parking areas for all our venues.
  • A complimentary, wedding night hotel stay for the bride and groom in one of our wedding suites.


To Learn More

If you would like to know more about our Reception Packages and available dates, please feel free to contact us and we will put you in touch with our wedding planners who will be happy to provide more detailed information about our reception services.
