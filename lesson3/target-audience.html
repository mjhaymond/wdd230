Target Audience

Our targe audience for the Chamber of Commers is single or married adult men and women between the ages 22-65. This age group is the working class with careers or the desire to create their own business. This is also the age group that has the income to travel and spend money at a variety of business.

Personas

Persona #1 - Loni Allen

Loni is 25 years old and is a personal trainer at a local gym. The gym is very crowded and doesn't have enough equipment to go around. Loni is thinking about opening up her own gym where so her clients can have better acess to the equipment they need to meet their excersice needs.

Persona #2 - Jack Cartwright

Jack is 28 years old and recetnly got engaged. He is excited to plan a dream honeymoon for his new fiance. He has some ideas of where to go, but he wants to make sure that he books the best and most reliable hotels, resturants, and excursions for the trip.
