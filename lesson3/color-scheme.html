Color-Scheme for Chamber of Commerce Website

The proposed color-scheme for the different elements on this website is a cool color palette with dark grounding neutrals. These colors have a classic feeling while still maintaing a modern vibe.
Webite Background: White
Body Background: #F0F7F4 - Mint Cream
Header/Footer Background: #477f83 - Ming
Navigation Bar:#99E1D9 - Middle Blue Green
Text in Navigation Bar: #32292F - Black Coffee
H2 Titles: #477f83 - Ming
Text in Header & Footer: White
Text in Body: #705D56 - Umber
